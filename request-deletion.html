Request Deletion of Your Personal Data

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from our systems. Please use the form below or contact us directly to submit your request.

What happens when you request deletion?

  • We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate
  • We will delete your personal data from our systems within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • You will receive confirmation once the deletion is complete

Submit Your Request

To request deletion of your data, please contact us with the following information:

  • Your account email address or username
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account and associated data
  • Any additional information that may help us locate your account

Contact Email: [Your Contact Email]

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request

Important Notes

  • Deletion of your data is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You may need to re-register if you wish to use the App again in the future
  • We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records for tax purposes)
  • Processing your request may take up to 30 days
